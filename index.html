THE WAY I SEE IT

I believe in a theatre of beauty and lightness where it's possible to tell great stories using humor.
Humor breaks down barriers, it's unique and different in anyone of us and it's a powerful tool for satire. I think that the best humor come from authenticity .

I don't think there is a "right way" of creating; when I embark on a new work the only tools that I can relate on are my playfulness, the complicitè with my group and a big pleasure in discovering and creating.
People that I met during my path and had a relevant influence on my creation and life approach are Philipp Schaeffer (TAKETINA rythm training) John Wright, Thomas Prattki (LISPA London International School of Perfoming Arts), Philippe Gaulier and Ariane Mnouchkine (Théâtre du Soleil). To all of them goes my appreciation and respect since they left me with more questions than answers.
